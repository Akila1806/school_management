# Natural Language Update Examples

The AI chatbot can understand and execute student data updates using natural language. Here are some examples:

## Email Updates
- "Update John's email to john@example.com"
- "Change Sarah Smith's email to sarah.smith@school.edu"
- "Set the email for student ID 5 to newstudent@example.com"

## Grade/Class Updates
- "Change John's grade to Grade 11"
- "Update Sarah to Grade 12"
- "Move all Grade 10 students to Grade 11"
- "Set Maria's class to Grade 9"

## Contact Information
- "Update John's phone to 123-456-7890"
- "Change Sarah's parent contact to 987-654-3210"
- "Set the address for John Smith to 123 Main St, City"

## Personal Information
- "Update John's father name to Robert Smith"
- "Change Sarah's mother occupation to Teacher"
- "Set John's date of birth to 1995-05-15"
- "Update Maria's gender to Female"

## Multiple Field Updates
- "Update John Smith: email to john@example.com, grade to Grade 11"
- "Change Sarah: phone to 123-456-7890, address to 456 Oak St"

## Bulk Updates
- "Change all Grade 10 students to Grade 11"
- "Update all students in Grade 9 to have parent phone 555-0000"

## Search and Update
- "Find students named John and update their grade to Grade 12"
- "Update all female students in Grade 10 to Grade 11"

The AI will:
1. Parse your natural language request
2. Generate the appropriate SQL UPDATE statement
3. Execute the update in the database
4. Provide confirmation of what was changed
5. Show the updated student information
